  • "Skorch"

    Skorch is a passon project that I have been working on for quite some time. It's an old-school style shooter inspired by retro FPS games like Doom, Quake, and Blood. It has a heavy emphasis on freedom of movement and technical skill. No release date yet.

  • Wretched Knell

    Wretched Knell is my death metal band. We have recently released an EP, titled "Death Evocation". Our influences range from the ripping florida death metal records such as those by Deicide and Cannibal Corpse, to the deepest depths of the swedish black metal scene, such as Dark Funeral, or the more melodic pieces by Dissection. We are working on our second album, and playing shows all around Ohio!
